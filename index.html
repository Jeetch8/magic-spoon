Hi Smiles Davis,

Over the last year, we’ve been quietly at work, pushing the boundaries of innovation not just in cereal, but also in cereal-eating implements. It’s our pleasure to unveil today the culmination of all that R&D, the ultimate in cereal eating tools: The Stroon!

Painstakingly crafted with cereal-eating and cereal-eating only in mind, the stroon combines the humble straw with the mighty spoon for a cereal-eating experience like no other…

We’re so proud of this, we’re rebranding in honor of this magical piece of cutlery.

And what’s more, for the next 24 hours, in honor of our new name, we’re giving away a free (real, yes) stroon with every single cereal order, so you can experience it yourself!

Stroons away!

Gabi
Magic Stroon co-CEO
